\section{Introduction}
Write one or two paragraphs where you describe the problem you try to solve, explain why this is a problem, e.g., planning for purchases of ingredients, preservation of the shop's offer, or a more general explanation why forecasting makes sense in many scenarios. In the latter case, the coffee sales just act as an example. Give a short pitch of how you treat the challenge.

\section{System/Solution/Architecture Overview}
Provide an overview of your solution. Use the figure you created for the last exercise sheet to explain your solution. You might adjust your figure to fit into the layout and to stay within the page limit. Include a short description of the forecasting approach you are using, ideally also providing a source.

\section{Demonstration Proposal/Scenarios/Workflow}
Provide a rough two to 3 sentences overview of this section. Never let a subsection start directly after the section heading.
\subsection{Setup}\label{subsec:setup}
If not done in the last section, provide a basic overview of your system, e.g. operating system, Database system and version, CPU, main memory, \textbf{even if you think this is not important.} There's always reviewer 2 who wants to know it precisely. If you want to show the performance of your approach, you might also want to go into more detail here.
\subsection{User Experience}
Describe what the user can expect when they visit your demo. This usually includes a walk through the features of the demo, example scenarios, a description of what the user will see, and things the user can do with the demo after an introduction. You may include a screenshot here. 

If subsection~\ref{subsec:setup} was not required because you described your setup earlier, you do not need any subsections at all here, just describe the experience the user can expect.

\subsection{Conclusion}
Make this a short paragraph where you summarize what your demo is showing and ideally it's unique selling point.

\section*{References}
References are excluded from the page limit, i.e., the bibliography might exceed the second page and it cannot be used to fill the 1.5 pages.
